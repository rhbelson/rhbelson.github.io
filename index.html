About Myself

With a background in networks & distributed systems, I joined Verizon’s Corporate Strategy team in October 2019 where I lead the 5G Edge Developer Relations & Experience team, focused on building the mobile edge computing ecosystem across solution architecture, field enablement, and developer engagement. In this capacity, I served as Editor-in-Chief of their Corporate Engineering Blog, designed developer tutorials & training modules and worked with large enterprise customers to solve their business challenges using automation, hybrid networking, and the edge cloud. Prior to Verizon, I was a Network Architect for bloXroute, a blockchain scalability infrastructure project. In this capacity, I supported a number of network planning initiatives, developing strategies to mitigate cybersecurity and DDOS risks.
